The Challenge of Pediatric Diabetes in India

Existing digital health solutions are failing our children, creating stress and confusion for families navigating this difficult journey.

Overwhelmingly Adult-Centric

Current tools are complex and lack the engagement needed to keep a child interested in their own health management.

Lack of Child-Friendly Resources

Critical health information is often presented in dense, clinical language that children cannot understand or relate to.

A Critically Underserved Niche

Pediatric diabetes management in India is a unique challenge that requires a focused, culturally aware, and empathetic solution.

A Friend for the Journey

Madhumeh Mitr combines cutting-edge AI with engaging gamification to transform diabetes management into an empowering and stress-free experience for the whole family.

Mitr AI Health Assistant

An intelligent companion for parents and older children, providing guidance and support in multiple languages.

  • ✓Multilingual Chat: Get answers to your questions in the language you're most comfortable with.
  • ✓Intelligent Reminders: Never miss a glucose check or medication with smart, timely alerts.
  • ✓Personalized Lifestyle Tips: Receive actionable advice based on local dietary habits and medical guidelines.

Gamified Educational Tools

We make learning about diabetes fun and interactive, empowering children to take control of their health.

  • ✓Sweet Balance: An interactive meal-planning game that makes learning carbohydrate counting an adventure.
  • ✓Insulin Hero: A narrative-driven quest where kids learn about the vital role of insulin in their bodies.

Our Bootstrapped Roadmap

We believe in sustainable, community-driven growth. Our development is funded by our users, not by investors.

Phase 1 (Year 1)

MVP Launch & Community Building

Develop and launch the core product on Android, onboarding our first 20 beta testers from the local Lucknow community.

Phase 2 (Years 2-3)

Refinement & Expansion

Refine the MVP based on user feedback, launch on iOS, and slowly add new features funded directly by subscription revenue.

Phase 3 (Years 4-5)

Profitability & Advanced Features

Once profitable, we'll explore adding more languages and advanced features like CGM integration, driven by user demand.
